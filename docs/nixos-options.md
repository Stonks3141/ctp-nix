## boot\.loader\.grub\.catppuccin\.enable

Whether to enable Catppuccin theme.

*Type:*
boolean



*Default:*
` false `



*Example:*
` true `



## boot\.loader\.grub\.catppuccin\.flavour

Catppuccin flavour for grub



*Type:*
one of “latte”, “frappe”, “macchiato”, “mocha”



*Default:*
` "latte" `



## catppuccin\.flavour

Global Catppuccin flavour



*Type:*
one of “latte”, “frappe”, “macchiato”, “mocha”



*Default:*
` "latte" `
